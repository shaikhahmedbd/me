FAQ

What is Live chat?

Through Live chat anyone can contact with you easily from the website. But you must have a Facebook page if you want to add this feature to your website. You can reply to your audience through Facebook messenger. 

What is call botton?

In your website there will a botton, from which any one can call you just by clicking on that botton. The audience don't have to type the number.

How can I control the contact form?

For most of my clients I recommend for going with google forms. Because it's easy to use and more secure than any others. If you use google form, you can control everything of that for through your mobile phone! But if you want regular form I can do that also. In that case, You can control the form by going to a website, which I will provide you while delivering the site.

What does responcive mean?

Basically responsive means anyone can easily visit your website without any issue through any device (mobile, tab, laptop, pc etc)
